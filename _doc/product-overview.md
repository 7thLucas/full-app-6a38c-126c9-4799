# Product Overview

## Working Title
**Laundry Ops** *(app name TBD — to be confirmed during onboarding)*

## The Problem
Multi-branch laundry owners rely on WhatsApp group chats to manage daily operations across all their locations. This means:
- Daily checklists are informal messages — easy to skip, impossible to audit
- Laundry status is communicated verbally, with no reliable record
- Photo evidence and problem reports get buried in chat threads
- The owner must manually chase every branch each morning just to know what's happening

The result: the owner is always reactive, never proactive — spending time collecting information instead of acting on it.

## The Solution
A focused daily operational monitoring app that replaces the WhatsApp workflow with a structured submission flow. Each branch staff submits their daily checklist, laundry status, photo proof, and problem reports through the app. The owner sees the status of all branches on one dashboard — no follow-up messages required.

## Target Users
- **Owner / Operator**: The multi-branch laundry business owner who needs full visibility across all locations without manually querying each one via WhatsApp every day.
- **Branch Staff**: Staff at each laundry location who submits the daily operational report — checklist items, current laundry batch status, supporting photos, and any problems encountered.

## Core Features (MVP)
1. **Daily Branch Checklist** — Structured checklist per branch; staff ticks off mandatory items before end-of-day submission.
2. **Laundry Status Tracking** — Per-branch status of laundry batches (e.g., masuk, dicuci, dikeringkan, siap diambil).
3. **Photo Evidence Upload** — Staff attaches photos to checklist items or problem reports as verifiable proof.
4. **Daily Problem Reports** — Staff logs issues with a description and photo; owner sees all open issues surfaced on the dashboard.
5. **Multi-Branch Owner Dashboard** — Single view showing all branches' daily completion status, open problem count, and laundry batch overview.

## Positioning
A practical, no-nonsense daily operations tool built specifically for multi-branch laundry businesses in Indonesia. Not a full ERP — just structured daily accountability and visibility, replacing chaotic WhatsApp group threads with a clean system the whole team can use.

## Tone & Brand
- Clean, practical, trustworthy
- Indonesian market; primary language: Bahasa Indonesia
- Calm and efficient — not flashy or consumer-oriented

## Scope Boundaries (MVP)
- **In scope**: Branch checklist, laundry batch status, photo upload, problem reports, owner dashboard
- **Out of scope (v1)**: Customer-facing features, POS integration, invoicing, scheduling, payroll
